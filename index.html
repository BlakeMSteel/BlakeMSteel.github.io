Do Starve

A roguelike based on hunger.

With Do Starve, I took the sample Unity 2D Roguelike game and made it my own. This included making the area bigger, tweaking the zombie and food spawn systems to make it more difficult, and completely revamping the wall placement system. Do Starve works on Mac, PC, Linux, and has controls for smartphone.

This project is open source. You can access it at any time here, but my work on the project is finished, unless something motivates me to continue. This is made with the Unity game engine, accessible here.

  • Date: May 2015
  • Purpose: Independent Study
  • Category: Game Design

ElevenDates

Tinder clone with Mario characters to learn Parse.

At University High School hired a consultant from 1150 to teach Advanced iOS App Development at their school. I, as a student of UHS, took this class, and this was one of the assignments that the teacher, Brett Keck, gave us to work on. It is a clone of Tinder using Parse to connect with Facebook.

This project is open source. You can access it at any time here, but my work on the project is finished, unless something motivates me to continue. This is made with Xcode, accessible here.

  • Date: December 2015
  • Purpose: Linking Swift knowledge with Parse
  • Category: Mobile App Development

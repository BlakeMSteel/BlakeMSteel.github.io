Do Starve

A roguelike based on hunger.

With Do Starve, I took the sample Unity 2D Roguelike game and made it my own. This included making the area bigger, tweaking the zombie and food spawn systems to make it more difficult, and completely revamping the wall placement system. Do Starve works on Mac, PC, Linux, and has controls for smartphone.

This project is open source. You can access it at any time here, but my work on the project is finished, unless something motivates me to continue. This is made with the Unity game engine, accessible here.

  • Date: May 2015
  • Purpose: Independent Study
  • Category: C# Unity
